About Sainan Zhang

Email: sainanzhang001@gmail.com  
Google Scholar, LinkedIn

Dr. Sainan Zhang, PhD, is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at NC State University and UNC-Chapel Hill. She specializes in wearable robotics, soft robotics, control, and human-robot interaction. Dr. Zhang earned her PhD in Mechanical Engineering from the City University of New York - City College. 

She has made significant contributions to the development of AI-powered exosuits and pediatric knee exoskeletons, particularly in their application to community-based mobility assistance. She has published 10 peer-reviewed in top-tier journals and conferences, including Nature, IEEE Transactions on Robotics, IEEE/ASME Transactions on Mechatronics, and Mechatronics. Dr. Zhang is actively involved in mentoring students and has served as a reviewer for several prominent journals including IEEE/ASME Transaction on Mechatronics, IEEE Transactions on Neural Systems and Rehabilitation, IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine, IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters.
